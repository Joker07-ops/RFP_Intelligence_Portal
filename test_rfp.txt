REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP)
PROJECT TITLE: Corporate Cloud Infrastructure Migration

1. OVERVIEW & SCOPE
The purpose of this RFP is to solicit proposals from qualified technical vendors to migrate our on-premise SQL databases and legacy web applications to a secure AWS cloud environment. 

2. REQUIRED DELIVERABLES
The selected vendor will be expected to provide the following milestone deliverables:
- A detailed cloud architecture design blueprint within 14 days of contract signing.
- Complete data migration of 4 production SQL databases with zero data loss.
- Post-migration optimization reports and 30 days of continuous infrastructure monitoring.

3. EVALUATION CRITERIA
Proposals will be reviewed by our internal steering committee and scored based on:
- Technical Approach & Architecture Feasibility: 40 Points
- Cost Efficiency & Milestone Pricing Model: 30 Points
- Relevant Past Performance & Team Certifications: 30 Points

4. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS
- Legal: The vendor must execute a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and carry a minimum of $2,000,000 in General Liability Insurance.
- Accounting: Invoices must be submitted monthly based on completed milestones. No advance deposit retaking options are available.
- Technical: All cloud architectures must comply strictly with GDPR regulations and adhere to SOC 2 Type II data security frameworks.
